In the duration-aware mixed-effects analysis, the abstract-versus-concrete effect in the left frontal ROI was not statistically significant after adjusting for log word count and ENEM item correctness (beta=3.629e-08, SE=2.221e-08, p=0.102262, 95% CI [-7.240e-09, 7.982e-08]). This suggests that allowing the trial window to vary with question duration does not by itself yield statistically significant evidence for a frontal condition effect in the current dataset.
