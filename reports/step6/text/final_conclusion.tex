In the trial-level mixed-effects analysis adjusted for log word count, the abstract-versus-concrete effect in the left frontal ROI was not statistically significant (beta=3.600e-08, SE=1.854e-08, p=0.052102, 95% CI [-3.276e-10, 7.233e-08]). This suggests that, after accounting for participant differences, question differences, and question length, the current data do not provide statistically significant evidence for a frontal condition effect.
